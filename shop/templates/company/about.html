About us

High quality Indonesian hand-picked fruits

Mangovodo uses fresh ingredients from our suppliers in East Java, Indonesia. Each plant is treated with upmost care and given the best nutrients for their growth, and to bring you the best possible products.
Browse our products.

Mangovodo established since 1976

Establsihed in 1976, Mangovodo has become the center marketplace for mango and avocado products purchases. Since then, our mission has always been to serve the best product in the market for our customers and to give them a pleasant experience while shopping. We want to share the joy of consuming avocado and mangoes to the whole world and also help those that are in less fortunate area with our products.

The Mangovodo Project

Mangovodo is a small e-commerce website project to hone my skills. This website uses the following tools:
django scss redis postgresql celery
In this project, I practiced the followings:

  • implement a responsive web design;
  • set up a user account system that tracks their orders and purchases;
  • set up asynchronous tasks;
  • set up mail system;
  • generate a PDF from HTML;
  • update the admin interface;
  • set up payment system;
  • containerize the project with Dockerfile and docker-compose;
  • and set up production environment with Gunicorn and Nginx
